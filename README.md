# Financial News Context

**Financial news, straight from the source.**

Financial News Context goes beyond replicating company press releases. We help readers understand what financial companies are actually investing in — the technologies, partnerships, markets, and strategies shaping the industry — and provide the context needed to see the bigger picture.

## What We Cover

We cover press releases from major financial companies including:

- **Banking** -- Bank of America, JP Morgan Chase, HSBC, Standard Chartered
- **Insurance** -- Allianz, MetLife, Zurich Insurance, Northwestern Mutual
- **Payments** -- Visa, PayPal, Stripe
- **Asset Management** -- Vanguard, Fidelity Investments

## Our Approach

- **Source-first** -- Every article links back to its original press release
- **Clear language** -- We cut through corporate jargon to deliver the facts
- **Timely coverage** -- News published as companies announce it
- **No opinion, just facts** -- We report what companies say, not what we think
